# Decision records

One file per consequential decision: `NNNN-<slug>.md`, numbered in creation
order. Sections: Context, Decision, Consequences, and Status (`accepted`,
`proposed`, or `superseded by NNNN`).

A decision earns a record when it is difficult to reverse or easy to
accidentally undo. Write the record before editing policy that depends on it.
Superseded records are never deleted; mark their status and leave them in
place.
